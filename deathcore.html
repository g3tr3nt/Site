Deathcore

Deathcore is an extreme metal fusion genre that combines the characteristics of death metal and metalcore and sometimes hardcore punk. It is defined by death metal riffs, blast beats and use of metalcore breakdowns. The genre is usually defined by breakdowns and death metal riffs or metalcore riffs played in the usual death metal tuning.
Popular deathcore bands in our days is Lorna Shore, Chelsea Grin, Whitechapel, Thy Art Is Murder, Fit For An Autopsy, Suicide Silence, Brand Of Sacrifice.
